Labels

Almost all inputs require a label tag. They are meant to clarify the purpose
of the input, and make the form flow logically.

  • Inputs that produce a button, i.e. the button tag, do not require labels but
    do require that the button has descriptive text as to its action
  • Clicking the label should give focus to the input this gives more targets
    to click on, as well helping users who fat finger buttons on a touch screen.
